About Me

Hi! I’m Fardouse(فردوس) — a software engineer, curious thinker 💭, and lifelong learner 🧠. With a BS in Informatics from the University of Washington, I specialize in software development and data science. I work across the stack and enjoy bringing ideas to life—from wireframes to MVPs—especially when it means learning something new.

I’m all about taking risks—even diving into the unknown—which is exactly what I love about building tools and systems with code. My friends would call me a risk-taker. I’m always down to learn new tech, tackle challenges, and team up with others to create clean, useful software that actually makes a difference.

Outside of work, you’ll usually find me at the beach watching sunsets 🌅, hiking, trying new restaurants with friends, adding places to my travel bucket list ✈️, or snapping photos of the sun and moon 📸🌙.

Thanks for checking out my website and I hope you enjoy! 🌺
